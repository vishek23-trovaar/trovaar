## Description (4000 chars max)
Trovaar connects homeowners and businesses with skilled local contractors — fast, fair, and stress-free.

**For Homeowners & Businesses:**
Stop calling around for quotes. Post your job in under 2 minutes — snap a photo, describe what you need, and watch local pros compete for your business with real bids.

- Post any home, auto, or commercial job for free
- Get competitive bids from verified local contractors
- Compare prices, ratings, and availability side by side
- Chat directly with pros before hiring
- Secure escrow payment — money released only when you confirm the job is done
- No surprises, no hidden fees

**For Contractors & Tradespeople:**
Browse jobs on a live map, bid on what fits your schedule, and grow your business without monthly subscription fees.

- See jobs near you in real time
- Send competitive bids to win work
- Get paid securely through the platform
- Build your reputation with verified reviews
- Manage messages, bids, and earnings in one place

**Categories supported:**
Plumbing • Electrical • HVAC • Roofing • Landscaping • Auto Repair • Moving • Cleaning • Carpentry • Painting • and 100+ more

Stop searching. Start finding.
